Our Goals

  • Reform education to prioritize critical thinking and science literacy
  • Establish a public healthcare option guided by health outcomes and cost-effectiveness
  • Combat climate change through clean energy, conservation, and green jobs
  • End homelessness and food insecurity in the U.S.
  • Get big money out of politics and restore public trust

How We Solve Problems

We use a framework rooted in science and data:

  1. Define the problem with measurable data
  2. Evaluate causes through peer-reviewed evidence and policy analysis
  3. Propose and test solutions with transparency and public input
  4. Adjust based on outcomes—not ideology
